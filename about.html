About Bryton's Auto Detailing

Hi, I’m Bryton. I’m a mobile auto detailer based in the Delaware and Montgomery County area — and I’m passionate about helping people take better care of their cars.

I come from a long line of car lovers on both sides of my family. Whether it’s under the hood or on the hood, I’ve always loved keeping a car in great condition. My own ride — a Ford Focus — gets treated like a showroom car, and I bring that same care to every customer I work with.

By day, I’m an insurance broker with State Farm, where I help people protect what matters. Detailing is my side business, but I take it seriously — not just because I enjoy it, but because it’s part of how I support my growing family.

In August 2024, my wife Clarice and I welcomed our first baby. She’s now at home full-time taking care of our family — and every detailing job helps make that possible.

So when you book with me, you’re not just getting a clean car. You’re getting someone who genuinely cares about doing the job right, every time. And you’re helping me do right by the people who matter most to me.

Let me take care of your car like it's my own. I look forward to working with you.

– Bryton Henry
